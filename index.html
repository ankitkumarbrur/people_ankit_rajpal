About Me


Beginning with the most recent, I have recently been awarded a patent on a watermarking tool that I have developed during my research. My patent provides a system for watermarking MPEG-4 videos. The novelty of the work lies in developing a new way to select the key-frames using the Fuzzy Inference System. Prior to this, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in computer science, publishing two journal publications and five papers in conference proceedings. My research interests include image and video processing, machine learning, and deep learning. I obtained my B.Sc. Honours and M.Sc. degrees in Computer Science from the University of Delhi securing the second position in B.Sc. and fourth position in M.Sc. I possess around 11 years of experience teaching at the undergraduate level. Currently, I am teaching at the postgraduate level at the Department of Computer Science, University of Delhi.
